Company Information

Our History as a TEAM

We are a newly formed company. Our existence came about in January of 2025 when our instructor put us together as a team for Experiential Learning class. Since then, we have thrived. We work very well together helping and encouraging each other in our projects.

We have created and configured all types of virtual machines; from workstations with different operating systems to many kinds of servers. We have set up a number of networks around the world and really enjoy the travel to different countries.


Why Choose Us?

We offer a combination of youth and experience that is unmatched. With our unique interview and testing procedure, we get the best of the best for employees. We are highly trained at Hawkeye Community College and have been through Chad's Experiential Learning Class.

We are experts in networking and working with servers as well as building powerful and efficient computers.

Every one of our employees have been through extensive training and internships that provide them with real world experience. Our techs are knowledgeable, good listeners and problem solvers.
